About

I'm a software developer based in Raleigh, NC, specializing in Revit add-ins and building information modeling (BIM) tools. I build software that helps electrical engineers work more efficiently inside Autodesk Revit — from panel schedules to one-line diagrams.

Outside of code, I'm a board member of the North Carolina Episcopal Church Foundation, where I help review and approve grant applications supporting communities across NC.

I live in Raleigh with my family. When I'm not building software, you'll find me fishing, playing Quiddler, or tinkering with my home lab.
